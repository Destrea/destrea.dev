Navigation Tutorial

How to Navigate:

• Click or tap the arrow at the top left of the screen, and select an option. This opens the associated window.

• Windows can be moved using a click and drag from their taskbar, and closed by clicking the in the top corner.

• All windows additionally have a button in the taskbar that contains information about the window, or how to use it.

• This window can be re-opened by clicking "re-play tutorial" in the settings window.
